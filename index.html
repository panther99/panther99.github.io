About me

I was born in September 1999 in Zvezdara, Belgrade. I was interested in computers and computer science from the earliest youth, long before I had my own PC. In 2012 I found a book about programming in C++ and that was the time when my excitement got even bigger. In secondary school I met Antonio, a friend from another class who was already working at that time and he assigned me few jobs. We also competed in Hacking Politics hackathon in 2016 where we won the second place with our project Polibot, which helps people to learn more about politicians in Serbia.

I competed in other hackathons too; CoderDojo Hackathon in Pančevo where my team won second place with HomePro prototype for smart home control over Messenger in 2017, SmartCity Challenge in Novi Sad where we also won second place with Pett, community oriented application for finding and homing pets. Most recently I competed in IT talents of the year competition organized by Metropolitan University where I presented EtarDnevnik, prototype of decentralized electronic journal for schools.
